# RunningBehind — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 1, 2026*

## Summary

RunningBehind does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data stays on your device.

## What the app accesses

**Location (CoreLocation)** — Used to calculate routes and track your journey in real time. Location data is processed entirely on your device. It is never sent to a server, shared with third parties, or stored beyond your local journey history.

**Calendar (EventKit)** — Used to suggest upcoming events as possible destinations. Calendar data is read locally on your device. It is never transmitted or stored outside the app.

**Maps (MapKit)** — Used to calculate walking/cycling/driving routes and display maps. Route requests are handled by Apple's MapKit framework under [Apple's privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## What the app does not do

- No user accounts or sign-in
- No analytics or crash reporting
- No third-party SDKs that collect data
- No advertising
- No data shared with third parties
- No data transmitted to any server

## Data stored on your device

RunningBehind stores your saved destinations, travel modes, prep checklists, and journey history in a local SQLite database on your device. This data is not accessible to other apps and is removed when you delete the app.
